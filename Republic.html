All you need to know about ’The Silver Age’and how to get ready for the new generation.

Written By Aishwarya Mammen | Mumbai | Published: February 12, 2024 18:09 IST

At a Glance:

  • ACSA epidemic which reduces the life expectancy for 25-30 years
  • New laws and bills passed this year by the Ministry of Human & Health Welfare
  • How can you be future ready?

Wondering why Aryan Khan and Arjun Tendulkar were not the only two who joined the Club 27? Scientists have been observing a pattern of people dying at the age of possibly 25 to 30 years. Last 6 months mankind has seen the highest death rate. Flight AI 636 of Air India was on course from Delhi to Bombay and it was carrying 854 passengers which is when Pilot Mr. Deepak have died of a severe heart stroke and in no time the co-pilot Ms. Manjima has also died killing 954 passengers on-board. Reportedly a total of 121,413,965 people have died in the last year.

This is identified as the ACSA Epidemic and this is being observed in people who are born around 2005 or after.Scientists have identified that Cyclophilin(Cyp) which is Peptide-propyl cis-transisomerase(PPDIase) grows in the human bodies of the first generation of the silver age. This chemical when grows to the full stage the body experiences death. An antidote served to this chemical at any time will lead to death by stopping the oxidation process of the body. "We have seen the first generation of 'The Silver Age', We should be ready for the future. This year onwards Child labour encouraged/ involved by an individual or an organisation which is considered as an offence under Sction 3(a) is now removed and added to Section 1(a). This year onwards children above years 12 are allowed to work as a labour or as a part of the workforce." Later that month the Bombay High Court released a Amendment where children above 12 years are entitled for employment in the public sector of India.

Ahmedabad, The fire department of the state has started training individuals of Male above 12 years. Other public sector departments has also satrted employing children to get them ready for the future. This training involves onboarding to the career of Fire services and the career plan needed for these children.

DHL, a courier service company includes employees of children above 16 years. This month the company will be the first one to employ five hundread first generation silver age citizens.

This epidemic has created a problem in families and relationships. While this panics everybody companies and governments are getting ready to face this epidemic. Inspite of accepting let's fight and find a solution. Last week UBER launched a relationship cell where experts help you in planning out your and your family's career if you are born in the first generation of the silver age.

While companies and organisations are helping their employees, The Ministry of Human and Health Welfare has come up with an In-Vitro fertilisation service accross the country. This bassically incntivises the parent whiever is ready to donate their sperm/egg. This is mainly focussed on benefiting women for surrogate motherhood. I would recommend you to either donate or carry the pregnancy. As per the plan the service is paying at an average of 40Lac per pregnancy. Following the government of India's bill which mandated every woman of the country. Recently The ministry of Human and Health Welfare has put up posters in University campuses and Educational Institutions.

How should you be future ready? 

If you are the first generation of the silver age then your children are possibly gonna suffer with their career and future. How do you secure your child's dreams when you are not around. Well, your parents or long living grandparents can take care of them. But who will take care of your grandchildren or what if your parents are not there to take care of your children. Republic TV has reserached the current possible solutions for insuring your children's life. Saving money for your childrn's future is the best way to deal with the silver age. Look at this bank called 'The Silverproof' which is the most consulted insurance company by everyone in the country. This Insurance company has 3 plans based on your convenience and eligibility:
1. Silver ULIP- This is the most opted insurance policy where a monthly investment results in 3 terms of premium return to your child. The first premium return is at 5years and it includes 20% of the insurance and the second return is at 7 years and it includes 20% and the third return is when your child turns 10 years old and it returns 20%. And when your child hits maturity at 12 years of age the remaining 40% of the premium i sreturned along with the bonus. Your nominee's gaurdian can reject any of the return at any age and it gets returned along with the final return.
2. Unit Linked Insurance Policy- This policy is especially for your child's education. This is linked in units and is returened at 10th standard, 12th standard, graduation of your child. This also has a final return at maturity.
3. Endowment Policies- This policy is best choosen based on the emergency requiement like Medical Emergency or for specific Educational degree or for your children's marriage.
